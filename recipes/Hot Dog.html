Hot Dog

Description

The Hot Dog is a staple in American cuisine. It is made simply by placing a pork or beef sausage inside of a Hot Dog bun and adding whatever toppings you desire.

Ingredients

  • Pork or Beef Sausage
  • Hot Dog Bun
  • Your choice of toppings. For example Cheese, Mustard, or Katchup

Steps

  1. Set Grill to medium, and grill your sausage of choice. Slowly turn your dogs for 5 to 7 minutes or until slightly charred and cooked through.
  2. Toast your buns on the grill to a desired amount.
  3. Place your sausage inside your bun and add toppings of your choice.
